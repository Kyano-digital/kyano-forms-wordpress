**SUPPORT**

Are you stuck in setting up Kyano Forms for your website? We provide free one-on-one support! Just send an email to support at kyano.app and we will help you with setting up Kyano Forms.
Do you have feature requests or ideas on how to make Kyano Forms even better? Please let us know! We love feedback and are constantly looking to improve our services.
Found a bug please report the issue through [Github](https://github.com/Snelwebcenter/kyano-forms-wordpress), and we will fix it ASAP. 

**PRIVACY**

The Kyano Forms plugin uses the CDN from [Kyano](https://kyano.app/forms/) to serve your the forms in the front-end. Kyano Forms does not interact with the visitors of the website.

You will find the terms of use of Kyano Forms on our [website](https://kyano.app/forms/)
